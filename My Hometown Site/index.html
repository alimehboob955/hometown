Visit Skardu

Enjoy natural beauty, cultural diversity and traditional dishes in this amazing city located in Gilgit-Baltistan province of Pakistan.

Top three tourist locations in Skardu

The Shangrilla Resort

The Shangrilla resort is located on the west corner of Skardu. It is one of the most visited tourist location.

The Manthoka Waterfall

The Manthoka Waterfall is located at a distance of 20 km from Skardu on the eastren side.

The Tormik Plateu

The Tormik Plateu is also a great place to enjoy nature .It is located at a drive of nearly 4 hours from skardu.
